ABOUT THE EVENT

ENET'COM's annual flagship event brings together prominent figures and over 2,000 students. A diverse range of multinational corporations and high-profile companies will be present, offering exceptional opportunities such as internships and job placements, with on-the-spot interviews as well. Furthermore, the forum serves as a hub for discussions, and the exchange of knowledge. The 8th edition of our forum is themed "ENE'TCOM STILL INNOVATES "
